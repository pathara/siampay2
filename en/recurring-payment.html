Value Added Services

Recurring Payments

Automate recurring payment
collection and billing in one platform.

SchedulePay is a recurring payment solution for SiamPay which allows merchants to facilitate automated billing and payment collection periodically. With SchedulePay, businesses no longer need to repetitively process recurring bills while customers don’t have to make manual payments on regular intervals.
Automate Payment Collection

SchedulePay removes the hassles of manually processing payments. Collect receivables from customers at regular intervals automatically.

Reduce Delinquencies

SchedulePay helps you receive payments on-time without the inconveniences and errors that comes with generating paper invoice.

Lower Costs & Business Risks

SchedulePay enables you to lower the costs associated with repetitively processing payments manually.

Features

 

Highly Configurable Platform

Easily setup future payments either weekly, monthly, quarterly, semi-annually or annually.

Seamless Integration

Add recurring payment functionality to your site within minutes. No need to edit any codes on your website, everything is done within our systems.
