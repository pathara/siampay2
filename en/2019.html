25 September 2019

Migrate to New 3-D Secure 2.0 Standard

3-D Secure (3DS) 1.0 has been around for years and creates an authentication data connection between digital merchants, payment networks and financial institutions. The new 3-D Secure 2.0 version (i.e. EMVR 3D-Secure) enables a real-time, secure, information-sharing pipeline that merchants can use to send more transaction information that the issuer can use to authenticate customers more accurately without asking for a static password or slowing down commerce.

Xecure3D is a product of AsiaPay Limited already got certified by EMVCo to provide 3DS 2.0 products that covers both merchant / acquiring bank side and also issuing bank side. This will cover credit card including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, CUP, Discover and Diners Club.
