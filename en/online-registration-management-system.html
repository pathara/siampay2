Online Registration Management System

Simplify online registration and payment processing on your website.

ORMS is an online registration system module which enables businesses, associations and organizations to facilitate online registration and payment processing for events, seminars and more. From online enrolment to international conferences, ORMS makes it easy for you to deliver a smooth registration experience for each client.

Maximize Sign-up Volume

ORMS makes it easier to register and pay for your services by providing a simple and secure online registration experience.

Modernize The Registration Process

ORMS allows you to reduce paperwork on registrations and manual processing of payments thereby improving business productivity.

Secure Payment Processing

ORMS grants your site the capability to safely accept online payments 24/7 through our PCI Level 1 compliant payment gateway.
